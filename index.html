1. Overview & Scope

Welcome to NagaBuzz ("we", "our", "us"), the premier news, bulletin board, and localized discussion platform designed specifically for residents across all districts of Nagaland, including Kohima, Dimapur, Mokokchung, Mon, and beyond. We are dedicated to providing a fast, engaging, and transparent environment that keeps you connected to local updates.

Your privacy is of vital importance to us. This Privacy Policy details the types of information NagaBuzz collects from its users, how we handle that information using our modern cloud architectures, and the robust controls we provide you to access or permanently delete your profile data at any time.

2. Data Collection

To enable personalization and community communication, NagaBuzz collects specific items of data. This falls under three categories:

A. Personal Account Credentials

When you register or sign in, we process information received from identity providers (e.g. Google Sign-In or Firebase Authentication). This includes:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your display name / username
  • Your profile picture/avatar (used as identifiers on comment threads)

B. User Generated Content (UGC)

NagaBuzz is a collaborative community platform. We securely store content you explicitly contribute, including:

  • Public Comments: Comments you submit beneath news articles, including timestamps and avatar links.
  • Notice Board Listings: Community announcements, job alerts, local examinations, and scholarship updates that you submit to the board.
  • Interactions: Article likes, view histories, and district-level feed filters (e.g., selecting Dimapur as your primary viewing district).

C. Technical & Diagnostic Logs

Our app processes system information to resolve crashes and protect our service. This contains device indicators, network types, and platform types (iOS/Android).

3. Backend Infrastructure & Storage

To deliver real-time comments, bullet-proof notice updates, and high-performance video playing, NagaBuzz implements state-of-the-art secure cloud database integrations:

⚡

Supabase Realtime Database

Used to sync news articles, comments, likes, and district lists. Data is structured, index-optimized, and hosted in regional enterprise-grade databases protected by row-level security (RLS).

🔐

Firebase Authentication

Manages user signup, session tokens, and authentication states. Your private passwords never touch our servers; instead, secure tokenized handshakes keep you safe.

All database instances are continuously audited and configured with restricted access nodes to prevent leaks, breaches, or unauthorized credential exposure.

4. How We Use Your Data

NagaBuzz does NOT sell, lease, or distribute your personal details to advertising networks or third-party brokers. Your data is used exclusively to power the following core application experiences:

  1. Personalized Local Feeds: Filtering jobs, exam results, and breaking news relevant to your preferred Nagaland district.
  2. Interactive Conversations: Enabling you to post comments, voice opinions, and upvote constructive discussions underneath headlines.
  3. Community Integrity: Moderating comment boards and verify submissions to prevent spam, scams, or illegal notice postings.
  4. Video Streaming: Adapting bandwidth feeds for high-fidelity YouTube and embedded video players within articles.

5. Your Control & Account Deletion

Under global privacy guidelines and modern App Store rules, you retain absolute ownership over your personal data. You have the right to request access to, correct, or completely delete your digital footprints.

Important notice about Deletion: When you submit an Account Deletion Request, NagaBuzz queues a complete wipe of your user record from both Firebase Auth and the Supabase database. This includes your profile details, avatar linkages, likes, and notices. You have a 30-day grace period where you can cancel the request by contacting us, after which the wipe is 100% irreversible.

You can trigger this action directly by clicking the "Account Deletion" tab in the navigation bar above and filling out our secure digital request wizard.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new features or security standards. We will notify you of any major adjustments by placing a prominent notice on the NagaBuzz home screen or updating the version date at the top of this website. We encourage you to review this portal occasionally to remain informed.
